About Me

Vivek H Sutar is a litterateur, writer, and poet. He has published various literary and non-literary works, with Healing Pages and Echoes of Emotions marking his debut. As the founder and president of Vatsala Mahila Sanstha, he tirelessly works toward driving positive societal change.

Born in a village in Kagal, Vivek completed his early education at Shantiniketan and Sanjay Ghodawat Cambridge International School, topping the 10th board exams. He pursued the International Baccalaureate Diploma Program during high school.

A national karate champion and former Vice-President of his school’s Student Council, Vivek initiated impactful programs like AFS, MUN conferences, and international university meets. His standout project, HealthForHer, spans 1,227 villages, aiming to prevent women's cancers. Collaborating with government bodies, NGOs, and leaders, the initiative has already saved thousands of lives.

Vivek has engaged with leaders like Sharad Pawar, Nitin Gadkari, and Sambhajiraje Chhatrapati, and works with Health Minister Prakash Abitkar to expand HPV vaccination statewide. His NGO fosters grassroots reform inspired by Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj’s legacy.

He is also active in agriculture, organizing large-scale tech events, and interning with cooperative industries. Dedicated to public policy, he now works with Samarjeetsinh Ghatge, Rajarshi Shahu's descendant.

Vivek is a dynamic young leader, known for oratory, impact, and vision—recognized by local governments and social leaders for his exemplary service.
